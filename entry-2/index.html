Symbol Garden

Discover the hidden truths by tracing the symbols in the coffee grounds

Bird: Birds represent good news, positive changes, or messages on the way.

Snake: The snake symbolizes caution, hidden dangers, or betrayal.

Heart: A heart represents love, romance, and deep emotional connections.

Tree: A tree suggests growth, prosperity, and long-term success.

Ring: A ring symbolizes marriage. If the ring is surrounded by clouds, it may indicate an unhappy marriage. If a letter is near the ring, it may be the initial of the future spouse.
